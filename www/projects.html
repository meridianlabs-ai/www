Inspect AI

Inspect AI is an open-source framework developed by the UK AI Safety Institute (AISI) with Meridian Research Labs as contributors. This framework enables systematic evaluation of large language models (LLMs), providing researchers, developers, and policy makers with the tools to conduct rigorous, repeatable assessments of AI capabilities and behaviors.

The platform enables standardized testing protocols to track model progress, identify potential risks, and ensure that AI systems are reliable and aligned with human values before deployment.

Inspect AI VS Code Extension

The Inspect AI Visual Studio Code extension, developed by the UK AI Safety Institute with contributions from Meridian Research Labs, brings the power of LLM evaluation directly into your development environment. This extension enables developers to quickly test AI behavior during the development process, without leaving their editor.

With the VS Code extension, you can detect potential issues in your AI systems early in the development cycle, ensuring more reliable and safe AI applications.
